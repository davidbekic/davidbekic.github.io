this is a simple 3D visualizer & tester for the Ã‰cole 42 project "push_swap".

USE:
1. generate n random numbers with the "fill stack" button.
2. copy the generated numbers to your push_swap (./push_swap copied numbers)
3. copy the stdout of your push_swap into the "input instructions" button. if you wish to generate many numbers you may need to pipe your stdout into a file.

PLAY
there is also a "play" button that allows you to manually put in instructions.

CURRENT LIMITATIONS:
- only positive input numbers.
- this page merely checks if your intructions sorts my generated numbers, so no leak/norm check or anything of that nature.
- the only way to see if it worked is to visually check if stack a is sorted.


CAMERA:
DRAG to rotate
SCROLL to zoom
SHIFT + CMD + DRAG to translate
